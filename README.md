### **About:**

Create: Food is an add-on for [Farmer's Delight](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/farmers-delight-refabricated) based on expanding food content found in Create, Farmer's Delight, and vanilla MC.

### **Required Dependencies:**

* [Create](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/create)
* [Farmer's Delight](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/farmers-delight)

### **Current Content:**

* Hundreds of new food-related items, including dozens of new fluids. View the item list [here](https://github.com/AverageAnime/create-food/wiki/Item-List).
* Various additions to enhance mod compatibility. Read more [here](https://github.com/AverageAnime/create-food/wiki/Compatibility).
* Version and mod loader differences are listed [here](https://github.com/AverageAnime/create-food/wiki/Version-Differences).
